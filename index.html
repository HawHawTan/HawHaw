Project

Know-or-Don’t is a concept of a note cards for students who want to study on the go. It has a simple and minimalistic look that keep you focus on the heading. It will prompt a question, if you know the answer, simply press the "know" button which display the answer and move you on the next card. If you don't, pressing the "Don't" button will reveal the answer before moving the card to the end of the list, where it will eventually come up again. Work in progress...
